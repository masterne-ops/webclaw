Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

Overview

webclaw ("we", "our", "the site") provides free online tools. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little data as possible. This page explains what is collected and why.

Data you enter into tools

Tools on this site are designed to run entirely in your browser. Text or files you paste, drop, or upload into a tool are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Server logs

Our hosting provider may keep standard access logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp) for security and abuse prevention. These logs are retained for a short period and are not used to build user profiles.

Cookies

webclaw itself does not set tracking cookies. Third-party services (see below) may set cookies in your browser.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • For more information, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Analytics

We may use a privacy-respecting analytics service to understand aggregate page-view counts. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Children

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@webclaw.com.
