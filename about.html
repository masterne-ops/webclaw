About webclaw

webclaw is a small collection of free online tools for developers and everyday productivity. We believe the simplest tools are the best — fast to load, easy to use, and respectful of your privacy.

Our principles

  • Privacy first. Whenever possible, every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your data is not uploaded, stored, or analyzed on our servers.
  • No sign-up. You should never need an account to format a piece of JSON or compress an image.
  • Fast. Pages are static and lightweight. They load instantly even on slow connections.
  • Free. Tools stay free. We support development through unobtrusive advertising.

What we are building

The first tools available on webclaw focus on common developer needs: JSON formatting and validation, Base64 encoding, timestamp conversion, and similar utilities. Over time we plan to add image and PDF tools that work fully in the browser.

Contact

For feedback, bug reports, or feature requests, please reach out via the contact email listed in our privacy policy.
